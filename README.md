# AdQuench - Free Water, Smart Advertising

A modern website for AdQuench, an innovative water kiosk business that provides free water to customers while generating revenue through advertising partnerships with small to medium companies, startups, and individuals.

# Business Model

AdQuench installs smart water kiosks in high-traffic locations and offers:
- **Free Water**: Customers get complimentary refreshing water
- **Advertising Platform**: Businesses promote their brands on bottles and kiosks
- **Targeted Marketing**: Reach thousands of daily consumers in strategic locations
